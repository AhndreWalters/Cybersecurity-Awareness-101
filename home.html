Welcome to Cybersecurity Awareness 101

Your first step to staying safe online.

Cybersecurity is the practice of safeguarding computers, networks, and personal information from unauthorized access and harmful attacks. In today’s digital age, no one is completely safe, whether individuals, families, or small businesses. This website provides an overview of common threats and offers clear, practical steps to help you stay protected online.

What This Website Is About

  • Raise awareness of the most common cyber threats.
  • Show practical steps to prevent attacks and scams.
  • Build safe digital habits for everyday life.

Why Cybersecurity Matters

  • Protects your personal information and privacy.
  • Prevents financial loss from fraud and identity theft.
  • Keeps your devices and accounts secure.

Call to Action

Ready to protect yourself? Explore common threats to see what dangers exist, then visit prevention tips to learn how to defend yourself online.
